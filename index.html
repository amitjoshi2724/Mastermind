Correct Sequence
Mastermind

Instructions:

This is the game mastermind. A sequence of colors is determined when the game starts. Colors can be repeated. You have to guess that sequence by clicking the colored circle buttons. Notice the undo button to be used if you make a mistake on a particular row. After you have guessed a row, ensure that you want to submit that row by clicking check. Once you have clicked check you can't undo that row. After check is clicked you will be informed of your results. A white peg means that one of your guesses was guessed correctly in the right location while a black peg means that one of your guesses was guessed correctly but in the wrong location. If a color is repeated in the answer, and a player selects that color, that color is only counted once by the pegs, or feedback from the computer. You have ten tries to guess the correct sequence.
